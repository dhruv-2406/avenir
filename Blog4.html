Home Automation: Personalization is the Key!

As Quoted by Cecelia Ahern “Home isn’t a place, it’s a feeling”
and how can one feel great when coming home doesn’t tend to be relaxing
and one has to switch and adjust Lights, A.C, Curtains manually after a
long tiring day? On other hands, a home welcomes you with pre-adjusted
temperature, perfect curtains, properly dimmed light and your favorite
music. Sounds peaceful, Isn’t it?
That’s when Home Automation comes into the picture.
Your home can be personalized to the level you have never thought of.
One of the most compelling features of home automation is the ability
to press one button that spawns multiple actions. Upgrading the
traditional switchboards with the personalized keypads where
your customized actions are engraved on it in your language,
pattern, button shapes and labels.

Let’s understand with an example, there’s a dedicated Home Theater in your home with beautiful Interiors with all the equipped technologies. From acoustics to the sound system to the screen to the projector. And with so many equipment comes so many remote controls. How about a single button in the entrance operate the entire room? With “Entertain” engraved on the button when pressed, all the curtains come down, lights are dimmed at 10% for first 2-3 minutes giving enough brightness for everyone to settle; AC temperature set at 24 degrees; projector, screen, speakers turning ON. You select your preferred movie/show and the lights turn OFF.
And thus, minimum 5 functions took place with a single button which otherwise would have taken 5 different remotes and an expert to control them all!
In another instance, let’s say it’s your bedroom, the button on the Keypad has been engraved for you as “Goodnight”. This will put your home to sleep with a simple touch. All the doors in the home will be locked, interior lights and televisions will turn off, the temperature is set back to a comfortable sleeping temperature you want, the alarm system will be armed, and the perimeter lights of home (the landscape lights) will turn on.
And just like that, multiple scenes can be activated. You can also create scenes for hours of the day, movie nights, reading sessions etc. Sky is the limit! Because it’s meant to be for You.
Scenes are single touch settings that set multiple functions at once and create a perfect one for You. It has predefined settings for lights, curtains, temperature, music, or whatever you choose. You can select and customize the functions yourself, to achieve the perfect ambient that suits your needs, from time to time.
Personalization options aren’t merely limited to devices in and around the home; homeowners can also quickly and easily set up triggers that send push notifications to their smartphones as well. Parents might choose to receive a pop-up alert when their kids unlock the front door at the end of the school day. Having Digital Video Door Phones is always a smart idea. You can see who is standing in front of your door when you’re enjoying your holiday in Europe. You can also communicate with them in real-time and unlock your home door for them if you want to.
Last but not the least, Smart home gives the sense of luxury, a luxury which can be seen, felt and appreciated. All of these things make for a better living experience in your home. When small things are being taken care of, less time is spent on adjusting the room as per your mood and more time with your family and friends, for your interest and comfort.
Home Automation is for You. It needs to Simplify things for You. It needs to lessen your tedious work. It needs to Understand You!
So, when you make your new home or renovate the existing one, make sure you have got a 100% Personalized Experience!
